Our Story

In the ever-evolving digital landscape, RevoU is rapidly growing to become Indonesia's largest live online learning community. RevoU's mission is not only to unlock opportunities and make a real impact on the lives of its students and alumni by providing the education they need to transform their careers but also to empower its employees.

From 10 to 10,000 students enrolled. From 1 program (Digital Marketing) to 4 programs (Digital Marketing, Data Analytics, Product Management, and Software Engineering), Kampus Merdeka, and B2B programs. From 5 to 500 industry experts as instructors and career mentors. From 2 to 150 employees fulfilling their potential.

Meet Our Founder

Matteo Sutto

Chief Executive Officer

In 2019, Matteo Sutto, with over a decade of experience in various industries and roles, founded RevoU. His goal was to create the best higher education platform in Southeast Asia and provide millions of people with access to better career opportunities. Under his leadership, RevoU has grown steadily in terms of students, employees, and stakeholders.
